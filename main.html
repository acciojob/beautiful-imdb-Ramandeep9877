A doomed car is the center of comic situations in this story that follows Ammy Virk, who belongs to a middle-class family of 8. His materialistic father is convinced by him to let him marry the girl of his choice. A small car is given by the girl's family as a dowry, and that pleases the father's greed. Yet, the car breaks when the 8 members of the family try to fit in the 4-seater.
